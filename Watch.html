On this page i will show you how a simple mechanical watch works!

This is a 5 minute video about how mechanical watches work i will sumerise this video into a short list!

  • Unlike in a quarts watch( which is powerd by battery) power in a mechanical watch comes from the mainspring which is stored in a mainspring barrel
  • The mainspring (which stores elastic potential energy) is wound by either turning a crown on the side of the watch and tightining the spring or by an automatic system which winds the spring at the movment of your wrist.
  • coming off the main spring is the center gear then the third gear, the second gear and finally the escape gear.
